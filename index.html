Things that shouldn't be done during Covid

  • Don't share your food with others.
  • Don't touch your eyes, ears and nose.
  • Don't come in contact with sick people.

Things that should be done during Covid

  • Wash your handfs for 20 seconds.
  • Wear a mask whenever you go to a public gathering.
  • Avoid crowded places.
  • Seek medical care as soon as you start noticing symptoms.

How to wash your hands properly?

1

Apply soap on hands

2

Rub soap from palm to palm

3

Cleanse the area between your fingers

4

Clean the back of your hands

5

Cleanse your hands properly with water
